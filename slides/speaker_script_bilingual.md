# Bilingual Speaker Script

## Speaker 1 | Slides 1-2 | Opening and Problem

### Slide 1

**中文稿**

大家好，我们今天的 presentation 题目是《亚洲女大学生校园健身房空间、身体意象与阻力训练回避研究》。我们的研究关注一个看起来很日常、但其实很有社会学意味的问题：学校健身房明明对所有学生开放，为什么很多女生仍然很少进入自由重量区，或者会主动避开力量训练？

我们的核心观点是，问题不能简单理解为“女生不想运动”或者“女生怕练力量”。在我们的数据里，很多参与者其实使用过学校健身房，也有稳定运动习惯。真正明显的差异发生在健身房内部：有氧区比较容易进入，自由重量区却被体验为一个更男性化、更公开可见、更容易出错被看见的空间。

**English Script**

Good afternoon everyone. Our presentation is titled *Spatial Environment, Body Image, and Resistance Training Avoidance among Asian Female College Students in Campus Gyms*. We focus on a very ordinary but sociologically important question: if campus gyms are officially open to all students, why do many female students still avoid the free-weight area or hesitate to participate in resistance training?

Our central argument is that this should not be reduced to "women are not interested in exercise" or "women are simply afraid of strength training." In our data, many participants had used the campus gym and many exercised regularly. The sharper difference appears inside the gym: cardio areas are easier to enter, while the free-weight area is experienced as more male-dominated, more publicly visible, and more risky for beginners who might make mistakes.

### Slide 2

**中文稿**

这页是我们的研究背景。校园健身房通常被想象成一个中性的健康空间，但空间本身并不中性。器械摆放、镜子、开放动线、谁经常使用某个区域、以及人们对“谁属于这里”的默认想象，都会影响学生是否真的敢进入。

与此同时，社交媒体也在不断生产关于女性身体的审美标准，比如瘦、线条、精致、自律。我们最初以为，社交媒体审美可能会强烈推动女生回避力量训练。但真实数据提醒我们，故事更复杂：审美内容确实会影响运动选择，但“怕练出肌肉、不够纤细”并不是本样本中的主要原因。

**English Script**

This slide introduces the problem. Campus gyms are often imagined as neutral health spaces, but space is not neutral. Equipment layout, mirrors, open visibility, who usually occupies a zone, and the shared assumption about "who belongs here" can all shape whether students feel able to enter.

At the same time, social media constantly produces ideals of the female body, such as thinness, tone, discipline, and visual attractiveness. At the beginning, we expected social media ideals to strongly push women away from strength training. But the real data tells a more complicated story: aesthetic content does affect exercise choices, but fear of becoming too muscular or not feminine enough is not the main reason in our sample.

## Speaker 2 | Slides 3-4 | Research Questions and Method

### Slide 3

**中文稿**

我们的研究围绕三个 research questions。第一，校园健身房的空间环境和 gym intimidation 如何影响女性大学生对阻力训练的回避？第二，社交媒体审美规范的内化在多大程度上塑造女性学生的运动偏好？第三，女性学生如何在健康运动目标和数字审美规范之间进行协商？

这三个问题对应三个层次。第一个是空间层面：自由重量区是否让人觉得不属于自己？第二个是媒介层面：社交媒体上的身体理想是否改变女生的训练选择？第三个是行动层面：女生并不是被动退出，而是会通过错峰、找同伴、换区域等方式协商进入。

**English Script**

Our study is organized around three research questions. First, how do the spatial environment of campus gyms and gym intimidation shape female students' avoidance of resistance training? Second, to what extent do internalized social media beauty norms shape women's exercise preferences? Third, how do female students negotiate between exercising for health and managing digital body ideals?

These questions correspond to three levels. The first is spatial: does the free-weight area feel like a space where female beginners belong? The second is media-related: do online body ideals affect training choices? The third is behavioral: women are not simply passive or absent; they negotiate access through timing, companionship, alternative zones, and other strategies.

### Slide 4

**中文稿**

方法上，我们使用小样本 mixed-methods design。问卷部分共有 34 人开始作答，其中核心有效样本是 32 名女性在校大学生。现在我们已经补齐了逐名参与者的文本版作答矩阵，所以不仅可以报告频数、百分比和题项均值，也可以计算个体层面的构念得分、短题组信度、Spearman 相关和探索性组间比较。

但是这并不意味着我们把研究变成大型推断模型。样本量仍然只有 32 人，而且部分组别非常不平衡，所以所有 alpha、相关和 Mann-Whitney 比较都只作为 exploratory evidence 来读。我们不做回归、中介或因果判断。质性部分来自 3 名受访者的一对一半结构式访谈，用来解释数字背后的空间情境和新手经验。

**English Script**

Methodologically, we used a small-scale mixed-methods design. In the survey, 34 people started the questionnaire, and the core analytic sample was 32 female college students. We have now added the participant-level text response matrix, so the analysis can go beyond frequencies, percentages, and item means. We can calculate individual construct scores, short-scale reliability, Spearman correlations, and exploratory group comparisons.

But this does not turn the project into a large inferential model. The sample size is still only 32, and some groups are highly unbalanced. Therefore, Cronbach's alpha, correlations, and Mann-Whitney comparisons are treated as exploratory evidence only. We do not run regressions, mediation models, or causal tests. The qualitative material comes from three one-on-one semi-structured interviews and helps us explain the spatial and beginner experiences behind the numbers.

## Speaker 3 | Slides 5-6 | Sample Pattern and Spatial Pressure

### Slide 5

**中文稿**

这页展示样本中的基本运动模式。78.13% 的参与者使用过学校健身房，46.88% 每周运动 2 到 3 次。所以这并不是一个“完全不运动”的群体。

但进入不同区域的比例差异很大。65.63% 的参与者最常使用有氧区，28.13% 使用固定器械区，只有 15.63% 最常使用自由重量区。同时，31.25% 完全没有力量训练经验，43.75% 只是有一点尝试但不系统。也就是说，学校健身房是可见的、可达的，但自由重量区并没有同等可达。

**English Script**

This slide shows the basic exercise pattern in our sample. 78.13 percent of participants had used the campus gym, and 46.88 percent exercised two to three times per week. So this is not a group that is completely inactive.

However, the use of different gym zones is very uneven. 65.63 percent most commonly used the cardio area, 28.13 percent used fixed machines, and only 15.63 percent most commonly used the free-weight area. At the same time, 31.25 percent had no strength training experience, and 43.75 percent had tried it only a little and not systematically. In other words, the campus gym is visible and formally available, but the free-weight area is not equally accessible in practice.

### Slide 6

**中文稿**

RQ1 的结果最清楚。空间压迫和训练焦虑构念的平均分是 3.72。最高的题项是“当自由重量区男性较多时，我会明显感到不自在”，均值 4.24，高分段 81.25%。其次是拥挤和器械集中带来的停留压力，高分段 75%。担心动作不标准被评价，高分段 65.63%。

补齐个体层面数据后，这一组题项的内部一致性是 alpha = .81，说明我们可以谨慎地把它们合成为“空间压迫/训练焦虑”构念。更关键的是，这个构念和主动避开自由重量区的频率呈 Spearman 正相关，rho = .65，p < .001。也就是说，在这个小样本里，越感到自由重量区有压力的人，越容易报告回避。

主动回避自由重量区也很常见。至少“有时”主动回避的人占 68.75%。回避原因中，“男性太多”和“不会使用器械”都是 59.38%，“不知道从哪里开始”是 50%。这说明障碍不是抽象的不喜欢力量训练，而是非常具体的空间和新手门槛。

**English Script**

The findings for RQ1 are the clearest. The average score for spatial pressure and training anxiety is 3.72. The highest item is "I feel clearly uncomfortable when there are many men in the free-weight area," with a mean of 4.24 and 81.25 percent in the high-agreement range. Crowding and concentrated equipment also create pressure to leave, with 75 percent in the high range. Worrying about incorrect form being noticed or judged reaches 65.63 percent.

With the participant-level data added, this item group has a Cronbach's alpha of .81, so we can cautiously treat it as a spatial pressure and training anxiety construct. More importantly, this construct is positively associated with active free-weight avoidance: Spearman's rho is .65, with p < .001. In this small sample, students who felt stronger spatial pressure were more likely to report avoiding the free-weight area.

Active avoidance is also common. 68.75 percent of participants avoided the free-weight area at least sometimes. The most frequent reasons were "too many men" and "not knowing how to use the equipment," both at 59.38 percent, followed by "not knowing where to start" at 50 percent. This suggests that the barrier is not an abstract dislike of strength training, but a very concrete combination of spatial pressure and beginner access.

## Speaker 4 | Slides 7-8 | Media Ideals and Interview Themes

### Slide 7

**中文稿**

RQ2 的结果比我们一开始预期的更微妙。社交媒体审美内化构念的平均分是 2.66，低于空间压迫构念。59.38% 的参与者同意社交媒体审美内容会影响自己选择什么运动，这说明媒介影响是存在的。

但是，“我担心力量训练会让我看起来不够纤细，或不符合主流女性审美”这一题均值只有 1.85，81.25% 在低分段。回避原因里，“不想练出明显肌肉”是 0%。个体层面相关也比较谨慎：社交媒体审美内化与回避频率的 rho = .31，p = .084，没有达到传统显著水平，而且明显弱于空间压迫与回避之间的关系。所以在我们的样本中，社交媒体更像是影响运动目标和选择的背景因素，而不是直接导致自由重量区回避的主因。

**English Script**

The findings for RQ2 are more nuanced than we expected. The average score for social media aesthetic internalization is 2.66, lower than the spatial pressure construct. 59.38 percent agreed that social media aesthetics affect what kind of exercise they choose, so media influence does exist.

However, the item "I worry that strength training will make me look not slim enough or not fit mainstream feminine beauty ideals" has a mean of only 1.85, with 81.25 percent in the low range. In the avoidance reasons, "not wanting to develop visible muscles" is zero percent. The participant-level correlation is also cautious: the association between social media internalization and avoidance is rho = .31, p = .084, which is weaker than the spatial pressure result and does not reach the conventional significance threshold. Therefore, in our sample, social media appears more as a background influence on goals and choices, rather than the main direct cause of free-weight avoidance.

### Slide 8

**中文稿**

访谈帮助我们解释这些数字。第一个主题是空间可见性。受访者提到，有氧区通常背对别人，而力量区有镜子，余光很容易注意到周围的人，也更容易意识到自己被看见。

第二个主题是新手门槛和动作错误羞耻。P3 说自己是新手，担心器械不会用、动作不标准，别人会觉得自己“好菜”。第三个主题是男性主导氛围。受访者并没有把男性个体描述为敌意来源，而是说当大多数使用者是有经验的男生时，新手女生会觉得自己和别人不一样。最后，访谈也支持我们对媒体影响的判断：受访者看到过“女生练力量不好看”的观点，但很多人并不认同，甚至会反驳。

**English Script**

The interviews help us explain these numbers. The first theme is spatial visibility. Participants said that in cardio areas, they often face away from others, but in the strength area, mirrors and open sightlines make it easier to notice other people and to become aware of being seen.

The second theme is beginner threshold and shame around incorrect form. P3 said she was a beginner and worried about not knowing how to use equipment or doing movements incorrectly, which might make others think she looked incompetent. The third theme is a male-dominated atmosphere. Participants did not describe individual men as necessarily hostile. Instead, when most users are experienced men, female beginners feel different in their experience, strength level, and confidence. Finally, the interviews also support our interpretation of media influence: participants had seen the idea that strength training is unattractive for women, but many did not accept it and sometimes directly rejected it.

## Speaker 5 | Slides 9-10 | Negotiation and Implications

### Slide 9

**中文稿**

RQ3 关注女生如何协商这些压力。我们发现，回避不等于完全退出。很多策略其实是“协商式参与”。问卷中，46.88% 会选择人少的时间去，37.5% 会只停留在有氧或固定器械区，31.25% 会放弃原本想做的力量训练，31.25% 会直接离开健身房。

访谈里也有类似策略。P1 会早上 9 点前去，或者找舍友一起去。P3 会不戴眼镜，让自己不那么注意周围，也会和朋友一起去。这些做法说明，女生不是没有运动意愿，而是在一个不完全自在的空间里寻找更可控的进入方式。

**English Script**

RQ3 asks how women negotiate these pressures. We found that avoidance does not mean complete withdrawal. Many strategies are better understood as negotiated participation. In the survey, 46.88 percent chose less crowded times, 37.5 percent stayed only in cardio or fixed-machine areas, 31.25 percent gave up the strength training they originally planned to do, and 31.25 percent left the gym.

The interviews show similar strategies. P1 would go before 9 a.m. or go with a roommate. P3 sometimes chose not to wear glasses so that she would notice the surroundings less, and she also preferred going with friends. These actions show that women are not lacking motivation. They are trying to create a more controllable way of entering a space where they do not feel fully comfortable.

### Slide 10

**中文稿**

实践建议上，最重要的不是告诉女生“勇敢一点”，而是降低进入门槛。问卷里最受欢迎的干预是女性初学者力量训练 workshop，均值 4.50，高分段 96.88%。这类课程可以教基础动作、器械使用、重量选择和训练计划，让新手知道从哪里开始。

个体层面的结果也强化了这一点：空间压迫/训练焦虑和干预偏好指数之间的 Spearman rho = .67，p < .001。换句话说，越觉得自由重量区难进入的人，越支持 workshop、半私密分区和减少刻板印象这些措施。

第二，空间可以更新手友好。半私密分区或视觉遮挡的支持率很高，高分段 81.25%。这不一定意味着性别隔离，而是可以设置入门练习角、减少一进入就被看见的压力、提供清晰的器械说明。第三，校园宣传应该少一点“变瘦”和“身材管理”，多一点功能性、多元身体和健康能力的叙事。

**English Script**

In terms of practical implications, the most important response is not telling women to "be braver," but lowering the entry threshold. The most popular intervention in the survey is a beginner strength-training workshop for women, with a mean of 4.50 and 96.88 percent in the high-agreement range. Such workshops could teach basic movements, equipment use, weight selection, and training plans, so beginners know where to start.

The participant-level results strengthen this point: spatial pressure and training anxiety are positively associated with the intervention preference index, with Spearman's rho = .67 and p < .001. In other words, the students who found the free-weight area harder to enter were also more supportive of workshops, semi-private zones, and reducing stereotypes.

Second, the space itself can become more beginner-friendly. Semi-private zones or visual screening received high support, with 81.25 percent in the high range. This does not necessarily mean full gender segregation. It could mean beginner practice corners, less immediate exposure, and clear equipment instructions. Third, campus communication should move away from only "slimming" and "body management," and emphasize functionality, diverse bodies, health, and capability.

## Speaker 6 | Slides 11-12 | Limits and Conclusion

### Slide 11

**中文稿**

我们也需要非常清楚地说明研究限制。第一，虽然这次已经补齐个体层面的作答矩阵，可以做信度、相关和组间比较，但样本量仍然很小，核心问卷样本只有 32 人，访谈只有 3 人，而且主要来自一个校园语境。

第二，有些分组很不平衡，比如最常使用自由重量区的人只有 5 人，所以 Mann-Whitney 比较只能帮助我们发现可能的模式，不能当成稳定的总体差异。第三，问卷题项是情境化自编和改编题，并不是完整验证量表，尤其干预偏好三题代表不同方案，不应被过度解释为单一心理量表。最后，我们不做回归、中介或因果判断。因此，本研究更适合被定位为探索性个体层面证据，而不是可推广到所有亚洲女大学生的结论。

**English Script**

We also need to be clear about limitations. First, although the participant-level response matrix now allows reliability checks, correlations, and group comparisons, the sample is still small: 32 survey participants and three interviewees, mainly from one campus context.

Second, some groups are very unbalanced. For example, only five participants most commonly used the free-weight area, so Mann-Whitney comparisons can suggest possible patterns but should not be treated as stable population differences. Third, the survey items are contextualized and adapted items, not full validated scales. The intervention preference items in particular represent different possible solutions, so they should not be overinterpreted as one psychological scale. Finally, we do not run regressions, mediation models, or causal tests. For these reasons, we position the study as exploratory participant-level evidence, not a generalizable conclusion about all Asian female college students.

### Slide 12

**中文稿**

最后，我们的结论可以概括为一句话：在本研究中，亚洲女大学生对自由重量区和阻力训练的回避，更主要是空间和新手门槛问题，而不是单一的审美内化问题。

这也改变了我们对校园健身房平等的理解。真正的平等不是只要设备向所有人开放就够了，而是要看谁能在这个空间里自然地进入、学习、犯错和持续练习。如果女生需要通过错峰、同伴陪伴、降低可见性来进入自由重量区，那么学校的健康资源仍然没有完全转化为真实可及性。我们的建议是：通过新手 workshop、同伴支持和更友好的空间设计，把“有健身房”变成“敢进入、会使用、能持续训练”。

**English Script**

To conclude, our main finding can be summarized in one sentence: in this study, Asian female college students' avoidance of the free-weight area and resistance training is mainly a problem of spatial and beginner access, rather than a simple problem of aesthetic internalization.

This changes how we understand equality in campus gyms. Equality is not achieved simply because the equipment is open to everyone. We also need to ask who can enter naturally, learn, make mistakes, and continue practicing in that space. If women need to rely on off-peak timing, companionship, or reduced visibility to enter the free-weight area, then campus health resources have not fully become real access. Our recommendation is to turn "having a gym" into "feeling able to enter, knowing how to use it, and being able to keep training" through beginner workshops, peer support, and more inclusive spatial design.
